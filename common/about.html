About BookMarket

We're on a mission to make books accessible to everyone by creating the world's most trusted marketplace for book lovers.

Our Story

BookMarket was born out of a simple frustration: the difficulty of finding affordable textbooks and the challenge of selling books that were just collecting dust on shelves. As college students, our founders experienced firsthand the financial burden of expensive textbooks and the waste of perfectly good books going unused.

In 2020, we decided to do something about it. We created BookMarket as a platform where students, professors, and book enthusiasts could easily buy and sell books in a trusted, secure environment. What started as a simple solution for textbook trading has grown into a thriving community of book lovers.

Our Mission

To democratize access to knowledge by creating a trusted, sustainable marketplace where books find new homes and readers discover their next great read at affordable prices.

Our Vision

A world where every book finds its perfect reader, where knowledge flows freely, and where the love of reading transcends economic barriers through community-driven sharing.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Trust & Safety

We prioritize the safety and security of our community through verified accounts, secure payments, and robust seller protection.

Community First

Our users are at the heart of everything we do. We build features and policies that serve our community's needs and interests.

Sustainability

By extending the life of books through resale and reuse, we contribute to a more sustainable future and reduce waste.

Education Access

We believe knowledge should be accessible to all, regardless of economic background. Affordable books mean better education access.

Innovation

We continuously improve our platform with new features and technologies to make buying and selling books easier than ever.

Passion for Books

We're not just a marketplace - we're book lovers building for book lovers, with genuine care for the reading community.
