Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I contact a seller?

You can message sellers directly through the book listing page. Click "Contact Seller" to send them a message about the book.

What if I have an issue with my order?

Contact our support team immediately through chat, email, or phone. We have buyer protection policies to help resolve any issues quickly.

How long does it take to get a response?

Chat support is instant, phone support is immediate during business hours, and email support typically responds within 24 hours.

Do you offer phone support in other languages?

Currently, our phone support is available in English and Spanish. Email support can assist in multiple languages.
