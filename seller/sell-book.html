Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about selling on BookMarket

How do I price my books?

We provide pricing suggestions based on current market value, condition, and demand. You can also check similar listings to set competitive prices. Remember, books in better condition and popular titles typically sell faster.

When do I get paid?

Payment is released to your account within 24 hours after the buyer confirms delivery. You can withdraw funds to your bank account or keep them in your BookMarket wallet for future purchases.

What if my book doesn't sell?

Your listing stays active until you remove it. You can adjust the price, update photos, or improve the description at any time. Our seller support team can also provide tips to improve your listing's visibility.

What books can I sell?

You can sell textbooks, novels, academic books, professional references, children's books, and more. Books should be in readable condition without excessive damage, markings, or missing pages.
