About BookMarket

BookMarket is Bangladesh's premier online platform connecting book buyers and sellers. Whether you're looking to find your next great read at an affordable price or hoping to give your pre-loved books a new home while making some money, BookMarket makes the process simple, secure, and enjoyable.

Founded in 2023, our platform was created with a simple mission: to make books more accessible while promoting sustainability through reuse. We believe that knowledge should be shared, stories should be passed on, and books deserve second, third, and fourth lives.

Our Vision

To create a world where every book finds its reader, where knowledge flows freely, and where literacy is accessible to all through affordable books.

We envision a community of readers and learners who share not just books, but ideas, stories, and experiences, making education and entertainment more sustainable and accessible.

Our Mission

To build the most trusted marketplace for buying and selling books in Bangladesh, with fair prices for buyers and sellers alike.

We are committed to making the process seamless, providing quality assurance through our approval system, and ensuring that every transaction benefits both parties while extending the life of valuable books.

Our Story

BookMarket began with a simple observation: university students were struggling to afford textbooks while simultaneously having stacks of books they no longer needed. Our founder, a university student himself, noticed this paradox and saw an opportunity to solve both problems at once.

What started as a small Facebook group for exchanging books among friends quickly grew into a campus-wide phenomenon. Students loved the idea of getting affordable books while also recouping some of their educational expenses by selling books they had finished using.

"Books are unique treasures - they should be passed on, not thrown away. Each book carries not just knowledge but a piece of its previous owner's journey."

As the idea gained traction, we realized that this concept could benefit people beyond just the university community. Book lovers, parents of growing children, professionals upgrading their skills - all could benefit from a platform dedicated to the exchange of books.

Today, BookMarket has evolved into a sophisticated online marketplace serving all of Bangladesh, but our core values remain the same: making books affordable, extending their useful lives, and building a community of readers who share not just books but a love of learning.

Our Values

Literacy for All

We believe everyone deserves access to books regardless of financial circumstances. By making books more affordable, we help spread knowledge and stories to more people.

Sustainability

By extending the lifecycle of books, we reduce waste and the environmental impact of producing new books. Reusing is one of the most effective forms of conservation.

Community

We foster a community of book lovers who share not just books but ideas, recommendations, and a passion for reading. Books connect people in meaningful ways.

Trust & Quality

Our approval system ensures that all books sold on our platform meet quality standards and are accurately described, building trust between buyers and sellers.

Education Support

We particularly focus on making educational materials more affordable for students, helping to reduce the financial burden of education.

Innovation

We continuously improve our platform based on user feedback, embracing new technologies and ideas to make book exchange easier and more efficient.
