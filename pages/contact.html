Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within Dhaka and 5-7 business days for other locations in Bangladesh. International shipping is currently not available.

What happens if I receive a damaged book?

If you receive a book that is damaged or doesn't match the described condition, please contact our support team within 48 hours of delivery. We'll help you resolve the issue, either with a refund or replacement.

How long does the book approval process take?

Our admin team typically reviews book listings within 24 hours during business days. You'll receive an email notification once your book is approved or if additional information is needed.

When do I get paid for my sold books?

Payment is processed within 3 business days after the buyer confirms receipt of the book. The amount will be transferred to your account balance, which you can withdraw to your bank account.

Can I cancel an order?

Orders can be canceled within 12 hours of placing them, provided the seller hasn't already shipped the book. To cancel an order, go to your Orders page and click on the Cancel button.
