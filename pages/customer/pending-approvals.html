Approval Process

How Book Approval Works

  1. Submit your book details through the Sell page.
  2. Our admin team reviews your submission (usually within 24-48 hours).
  3. If approved, you'll need to pay a 5% commission fee.
  4. After payment, your book will be listed on the platform.
  5. If rejected, you'll receive a notification with the reason.

Important Note

You can edit your book details while it's in "Pending" status, but not once it's "Under Review". If your book is rejected, you can make the suggested changes and resubmit.
